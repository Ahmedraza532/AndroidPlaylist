Supercars are high-performance,
luxury sports cars known for their speed, power, and advanced technology.
They often feature sleek, aerodynamic designs and are equipped with powerful engines that can produce over 500 horsepower.
Supercars are typically produced in limited quantities by prestigious manufacturers like Ferrari, Lamborghini, and McLaren.
These vehicles are not just about speed; they also offer superior handling, braking, and overall driving experience.
Owning a supercar is often seen as a status symbol due to their high price and exclusivity.
